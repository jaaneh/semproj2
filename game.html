How to play Just Snakes

Basic Information

As this game is meant for 2 players, you'll start with selecting your characters. When the 2 players have been selected, you'll be promted to go to the game.

The board has a total of 30 tiles, first to reach tile 30, wins!

Trap Tiles

The board consists of 30 tiles total, but be careful! If you happen to get unlucky and land on any of the 5 trap tiles, you will be moved back 2-4 tiles depending on the trap.

Throwing Sixes

When a player throws a six on the dice, that player will get an extra throw. If a player throws 2 sixes, they will move 12 tiles (if they didn't hit a trap), and the next player will get their turn.

With some luck, JustSnakes games can be over very quickly, so you better hope you roll some sixes.

A player can maximum have 2 throws, then the game will allow the next player to have their turn.

Missing Characters

Looks like you didn't select your characters. Please go back and select 2 characters to play the game.
